---
name: content-blog
description: Turn source material, notes, or transcripts into a practical blog draft using the configured writing style.
---

Use this skill for blog drafting and blog edits.

Workflow:

1. Use `$writing-style` first.
2. Read available source material from `source/` and `content/`.
3. Identify the actual thesis before drafting.
4. Produce or edit `content/blog.md` or the repo's established blog file.
5. Keep structure useful: clear opening, developed argument, concrete examples, tight ending.

Do not pad. Do not turn notes into generic marketing copy.
