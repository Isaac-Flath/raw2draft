---

Use this skill for social scheduling.

Workflow:

1. Inspect the project's `social/` files and related blog/content files.
2. Check whether each asset is ready, missing context, or needs editing.
3. Suggest a compact posting plan with exact dates or relative order.
4. Keep copy and scheduling notes separate.
