---
name: video-editor
description: Plan or prepare video editing work for a Raw2Draft content project.
---

Use this skill for video editing tasks that do not require direct DaVinci Resolve automation.

Workflow:

1. Inspect source video, transcript, screenshots, and project notes.
2. Identify the desired cut, clips, overlays, or export target.
3. Prefer existing repo scripts for extraction, subtitles, and video assets.
4. Produce concrete edit instructions or run safe local prep commands.
